
🔗[Problem link](https://leetcode.com/problems/trapping-rain-water/description/)
Given array of positive integers representing an elevation map where the width of each bar is 1, compute how much water it can trap after raining.

---

### 🧠Intuition

![](https://assets.leetcode.com/uploads/2018/10/22/rainwatertrap.png)

**Input:** height = [0,1,0,2,1,0,1,3,2,1,2,1]
**Output:** = 6

 - KEY CONCEPT : We don't have to “check everything between two bars” ***instead*** we need to check- **How much water sits on top of _each_ bar** (not between two bars).
- And that water is `min(max_left, max_right) - height[i]` for each bar.
- This ca be done by 2 approaches - 
     1. Using 2 arrays for maxleft (maxprefix)  & maxright (maxsuffix).  SC - O(2N)
     2. Using 2 Pointers to store max left and max right. SC - O(1)
